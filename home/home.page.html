In Astral, author Cale Madison delivers a mysterious and intriguing adventure of a sorcerer finding his purpose in a precarious world. The discovery of a transcendent being, the Ancient One, yields unabating wars between kingdoms, propelling this sorcerer into a dangerous game with sinister characters and unimaginable horrors.

At the center of this conflict is Astrum, Academy of the Stars, a college of sorcery and haven for lost magicians. The scholars there prepare for a cosmic war against the Ancient One, who has traveled eons to devour their sun. Amidst his graduating year, Rin discovers that terrors are amassing beyond their walls — assassins, celestial monsters, soldiers and supernatural forces.

In a land not so far away, the acolytes of House Harkin worship the encroaching god and plot retaliation against Astrum. Their ruthless and unyielding leader leads an assault on the academy, gathering armies from across the brutal northlands to join their crusade.

The fate of the Harkins and the scholars of Astrum hangs perilously in the balance. The deadliest of conflicts is about to begin…and only one young hero is capable of thwarting oblivion.
